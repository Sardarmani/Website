1. Company Overview & Market Position

Company Profile

Dolmen City REIT (DCR) is Pakistan's first and only listed commercial REIT, launched in June 2015 by Arif Habib Dolmen REIT Management Limited (AHDRML). As a perpetual, closed-end, Shariah-compliant rental REIT, DCR was established under SECP regulations to generate stable rental income from premium commercial real estate assets. The REIT is managed by a professionally qualified team with extensive real estate experience within the Arif Habib Group, one of Pakistan's oldest and most reputable real estate developers and investment firms.
