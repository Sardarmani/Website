FAQ

+

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive data science and machine learning services including predictive modeling, data analysis, deep learning solutions, computer vision, natural language processing, and business intelligence. We also provide end-to-end project development from data collection to model deployment.

+

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies based on complexity and scope. Simple data analysis projects may take 1-2 weeks, while complex machine learning models with custom algorithms can take 1-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the project.

+

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to large enterprises. We offer flexible engagement models including hourly consulting, project-based work, and ongoing support contracts. We're particularly experienced in helping small businesses leverage data science to grow their operations.

+

What technologies do you use?

We work with Python, R, TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, pandas, NumPy, and various cloud platforms. We're also experienced with SQL, NoSQL databases, and deployment technologies like Docker and AWS. We stay updated with the latest tools and frameworks in the data science ecosystem.

+

How do I get started?

Simply contact us through the contact form or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your needs, discuss your project requirements, and provide a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing. We're available for both one-time projects and ongoing partnerships.
